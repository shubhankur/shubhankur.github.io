01 / The product

Sweezy sits at the sweet spot between an AI assistant and AI Friend.

Sweezy mimics the real world assistant and not an employee. Like Donna to Harvey and Alfred to Batman.

Assistant

The simplest AI assistant that closes the loop. You say a few words and she builds your daily plan. The next day when you show up, she asks about your day and how much you got done. She holds you accountable and marks your progress, not just an endless list of overdue tasks. She also records the conversation about your day as a journal entry without you even realizing it.

Friend

A real friend. IRL you don't always meet people to get something done, you just go out in the evening. That's what Sweezy is. You just show up for 5 minutes every evening. In a few weeks, she will know you more than anyone else in the world. The next time you tell her, you are feeling down, she will tell you whan you need to hear and not what you want.
