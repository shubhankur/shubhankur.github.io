01 / Sweezy

Sweezy is the daily life companion the world has been trying to build.

The wedge is simple: a five-minute voice ritual. The product turns conversation into plan, journal, goals, reminders, calendar structure, and memory that compounds over time.

Sweezy: Daily Life Companion

iOS app. Version 2.2.0. Updated May 1, 2026.

Most AI talks. Sweezy runs your day because she knows your life.

~2,000 downloads from one Reddit launch post
~300 onboarded users in the early loop
5.0 App Store rating across 7 public ratings
Ritual

Daily, not reactive

ChatGPT waits for a prompt. Sweezy is built around a recurring moment in the day: show up, talk, leave with structure.

Memory

Structured, not flat

Goals, plans, journals, weekly activity, patterns, and profile context are product state, not a loose list of remembered facts.

Artifacts

Useful receipts

The output is not just a reply. It is a plan you can follow, a journal you can revisit, goals you can track, and calendar structure you can live inside.

Standing

Pushback earns trust

The prototype moment is Sweezy refusing an unrealistic work block because she had seen the user fail that pattern before.

05 / Why me

I am built for this specific company, not just startups in general.

I grew up in Hajipur, Bihar, where startups and tech were not normal conversation. That distance matters. Sweezy is not being built only for people already fluent in AI. It is for people who need AI to meet them where they are.

My edge is the combination: local business operator, production security engineer, immigrant founder, and obsessive user of the problem. I know the pain of needing someone with full context and not having one.

That is why Sweezy is not a small ChatGPT. It is a daily ritual with memory, artifacts, accountability, and eventually action across the apps that run your life.
